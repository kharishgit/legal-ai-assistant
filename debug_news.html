Husband can't be punished under Section 377 IPC for anal or oral sex with wife: Delhi High Court

The Court said the law presumes a wife's implied consent for sexual intercourse and sexual acts including anal and oral sex in a marriage.
Delhi High Court
Ratna Singh
Published on: 
22 May 2025, 8:20 am
3 min read
  • Copied

The Delhi High Court recently held that Section 377 of the Indian Penal Code (IPC) criminalising unnatural sexual offences cannot be invoked to punish a husband for anal or oral sex with his wife.
